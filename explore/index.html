Travel Essentials For Nepal

Permits and Entry

Before long routes, double-check visa duration, permit validity, and printed backups. Remote checkpoints still appreciate physical copies.

Safety and Health

For altitude itineraries, build in acclimatization days and stay flexible. A slower plan often means a better and safer trip.

Connectivity and Payments

Offline maps and small cash notes save time in hill districts. Download bookings and emergency contacts before leaving major cities.

Featured Nepal Circuits

Heritage Circuit

Kathmandu Valley, Janakpur, and Lumbini for architecture, rituals, and living history you can feel street by street.

Mountain Circuit

Pokhara, Annapurna gateways, and Everest foothills for trails, sunrise viewpoints, and long mountain horizons.

Wildlife Circuit

Chitwan, Bardiya, and Shuklaphanta for jungle safari days, wetland birding, and quieter grassland reserves.
